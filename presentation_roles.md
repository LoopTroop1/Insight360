# SIH 2026: 6-Person Hackathon Presentation Guide
*Pitching "e-Office Pro" to the Jury: Responsibilities, Visual Demos, and Key Scripts*

---

## 🎤 Speaker 1: The Project Anchor (Problem & Vision)
*   **Role**: Team Leader / Main Presenter.
*   **Focus**: Problem definition, management gap, and the overarching vision of e-Office Pro.
*   **What to Demo on Screen**: The **Login Gateway** (`/`).
*   **Key Talking Points**:
    *   *The Problem*: e-Office tracks files, but has **no tools** to monitor, evaluate, or benchmark productivity at the **Organizational, Team, and Individual levels**.
    *   *The Solution*: e-Office Pro overlays a real-time performance layer to calculate the Departmental Productivity Index ($DPI$) and protect employee well-being.
*   **Catchphrase**: *"We aren't replacing e-Office; we are giving it a brain to benchmark success and prevent silent burnout."*

---

## 💻 Speaker 2: The UI/UX Architect (Frontend & Workspaces)
*   **Role**: Frontend Lead.
*   **Focus**: Premium aesthetics, design consistency, and responsive role-based workspaces.
*   **What to Demo on Screen**: Switch profiles using the **Persona Switcher** to show the **Officer Dashboard** (`/officer`), **Team Lead View** (`/teamlead`), and **Secretary View** (`/secretary`).
*   **Key Talking Points**:
    *   *Design System*: Implemented a GovTech design system using Outfit & Inter typography with clean, micro-animated widgets.
    *   *Role Separation*: Section Officers see their pending file queues and weekly progress charts; Team Leads see team stress levels; Cabinet Secretaries see National goals.
*   **Catchphrase**: *"One unified portal, three specialized personas, designed for high-speed administrative operation."*

---

## 🧠 Speaker 3: The AI & Modeling Lead (Decision Center & Twin)
*   **Role**: Data Scientist / AI Developer.
*   **Focus**: Mathematical engines, explainable AI, and Digital Twin projections.
*   **What to Demo on Screen**: The **AI Decision Center** (`/ai-center`) — move the backlog slider and show the projected DPI before executing.
*   **Key Talking Points**:
    *   *The Engine*: Explain the weighted $DPI$ formula and the **Explainable AI (XAI)** delay risk factors.
    *   *Digital Twin*: Superiors can model queue transfers in real-time, forecasting backlog distributions *before* dispatching files.
*   **Catchphrase**: *"We do not use 'black-box' models. Our AI is fully explainable and computes in less than a millisecond directly on the edge."*

---

## 🛡️ Speaker 4: The Security & Trust Engineer (Audit Vault)
*   **Role**: Cybersecurity & Database Lead.
*   **Focus**: Tamper-evident ledger logging and Aadhaar-linked digital signature checks.
*   **What to Demo on Screen**: The **Audit Vault** (`/audit-vault`) page — click **Verify Ledger Integrity** to show the green verification alert.
*   **Key Talking Points**:
    *   *Cryptographic Integrity*: Every action (file forwards, digital signings) is chained sequentially using SHA-256 block hashing. If a database record is tampered with, the chain breaks instantly.
    *   *Aadhaar Verification*: Simulates secure 2FA authentication on notesheet approvals.
*   **Catchphrase**: *"Every notesheet approval is cryptographically anchored. We guarantee a completely tamper-evident administrative history."*

---

## 🔌 Speaker 5: The Backend & Integrations Lead (APIs & SPARROW Sync)
*   **Role**: Systems / Devops Lead.
*   **Focus**: Database schemas, backend endpoints, and G2G data syncing.
*   **What to Demo on Screen**: The **SPARROW Sync** (`/sparrow-sync`) page — show the live XML schema output and trigger sync.
*   **Key Talking Points**:
    *   *Architecture*: A Next.js App Router API backbone querying SQLite using Prisma ORM.
    *   *Interoperability*: Compiled performance indicators dynamically convert to the official G2G (Government-to-Government) XML schema to sync with national appraisal systems.
*   **Catchphrase**: *"Our database matches real government data schemes, making e-Office Pro plug-and-play with existing NIC infrastructure."*

---

## 📊 Speaker 6: The QA & Verification Lead (Build & Deliverables)
*   **Role**: Release / Quality Assurance Lead.
*   **Focus**: Code quality, test metrics, and automated reporting.
*   **What to Demo on Screen**: The **Print Reports** (`/reports`) page to download a printable PDF report, then log out.
*   **Key Talking Points**:
    *   *Build Quality*: The codebase compiles cleanly into static and server-rendered assets with zero warnings or errors.
    *   *Analytics Reporting*: Generates printable summaries of department ranks, backlog improvements, and individual achievements.
*   **Catchphrase**: *"From secure login to final appraisal report download, the entire loop is tested, validated, and optimized for production."*
